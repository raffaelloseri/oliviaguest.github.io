I am a professor of Econometrics at the Department of Economics of the Università degli Studi dell'Insubria. I am also a member of the Center for Nonlinear and Complex Systems in the same university and of the Centre for Computational & Organisational Cognition in the University of Southern Denmark.

I have a Laurea and a PhD in Industrial Engineering from Politecnico di Milano, a DEA in Mathematics applied to Social Sciences from Université Paris Dauphine/ENSAE and a PhD in Mathematics from Université Paris Dauphine.

I am coordinator of the major in Economics of Innovation in the MSc Global Entrepreneurship Economics and Management (GEEM) of the Università degli Studi dell'Insubria.
